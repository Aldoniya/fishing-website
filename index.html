About Our System

GIS & Remote Sensing for Fishing

Our platform uses Geographic Information System (GIS) and Remote Sensing (RS) technologies to identify and predict fishing hotspots in Zanzibar waters.

By analyzing satellite imagery, ocean conditions, water temperature, and historical fishing data, we provide accurate recommendations for the best fishing locations.

  • Real-time satellite data analysis
  • Historical fish migration patterns
  • Ocean temperature monitoring
  • Weather condition integration
